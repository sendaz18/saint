Our Origin Story

Cloud experts driving smart, scalable solutions for a digital-first world.

Our company is driven by a love for innovation, striving to make good on every opportunity given to us by delivering cutting-edge cloud-based solutions that transform industries.

Naleko Digital Solutions was born out of collaboration, resilience, and a shared passion for technology. In 2020, during the height of remote work adaptation, a Software Developer, a Software Engineer, and a Business Analyst created a WhatsApp group to support one another. What started as casual peer support quickly blossomed into something far greater, a vision to transform the digital landscape. And so, Naleko Digital Solutions was founded.

Today, we are a fast-growing IT software company with a strong focus on cloud computing, CRM transformation, and intelligent application development. We empower businesses across healthcare, finance, retail, banking, and insurance to elevate customer experiences and streamline operations through scalable, cloud-first strategies.

At the heart of our services lies Salesforce, enabling smarter customer management, AI-powered insights, and stronger relationships. We’ve since expanded our technology stack to include a range of cloud platforms, broadening our impact and adapting to the ever-evolving needs of modern enterprises.
